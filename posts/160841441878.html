Kenneth Williams diaries, on “Carry on Cleo”

Tuesday, 4th August 1964.

This Roman tunic I’m wearing for the film is really quite attractive. In white and gold. I continually lift it up and expose my cock & everything at the Unit. They’re all rather disgusted and laugh it off, but quite a number of them have remarked ‘O! Kenny! Not again! – put it away …’ etc. etc. 

And all our trousers shall be taken down and used in evidence against us.
